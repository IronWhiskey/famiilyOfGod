Missions



 

Hope 101 is a relational transitional housing program which was birthed by Family of God Community Church and is now a 501 (c)(3) non-profit organization. Newaygo County has a great unmet need for transitional housing.

Hope 101 Ministry Inc. is a self-sufficiency program in Newaygo, Michigan, which provides up to six months housing for homeless persons who are willing to set goals and do the work necessary to become self-sufficient.  Referrals are made by area social service agencies, churches, schools, or individuals.  A professional case manager screens applicants and supports program participants as they set goals and work to accomplish them. The case manager can be reached at 231-245-8877 or casemanager@hope101ministry.com

Related services are provided by various agencies, members of our congregation, and the community. These include professional counseling, employment assistance and counseling, parenting guidance, financial planning and budgeting, personal support and mentoring and other services as needed. Each adult resident has one or two team mentors to walk alongside them. Participants are invited to participate in activities of Family of God Community Church but there is be no pressure or expectation that they will do so.

HOPE 101 began as a ministry of Family of God Community Church to connect our passion to provide safe places to live for our brothers and sisters in Christ locally and globally. This housing ministry speaks to our mission of putting love in action, by welcoming, witnessing and worshiping. May this purpose continue to define who we are and give us an identity in the Newaygo community that will help us be the family of God.

Hope 101 is generously supported by the Fremont Area Community Foundation.
