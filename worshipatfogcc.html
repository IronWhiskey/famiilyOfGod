Be Our Guest on Sunday mornings!

What is the atmosphere like?
At Family of God Community Church, you will find real people.  Our mission statement says that we are a welcoming, diverse body of believers!  That means, that ALL are welcome—after all, who would not be welcome to be a part of the family of God?   Our church likes to say that we are all about welcome, worship and witness—and we hope that you will help us do that better!  When people are asked to describe FOGCC, they use words like warm-hearted, friendly, real, open and loving—they often say it feels like we are family! 

What are some features of the worship service?

  • A time of worship that attempts to offer something for everyone—regardless your age or stage
  • A time of fellowship to connect with other people
  • A message by our pastor that is inspirational, practical, and relevant

What are the messages like?
Our pastor preaches messages that are based on the Bible, yet practical and relevant to every day life. Whether you've been a follower of Jesus Christ for years or you are not familiar with the Bible, you'll understand the messages and be able to apply them to your life. Check out a sermon or two—look in the right column for the “SERMONS” tab.

How long does the service last?

Our worship service usually lasts about 60-90 minutes.  This is followed by a time of fellowship—and then, we offer C.A.R.E. (Child and Adult Religious Education—a..k.a. Sunday School) that meets from 11:25 to 12:10 during the school year.   

What is the attire?
The attire is respectfully causal. Jeans are fine!
